WORK, STUDY, ACCESSIBILITY – these three words capture the vision of the Work Study Program at North House Folk School. It is an opportunity to work in a program support/community service role on campus while earning credit towards taking an upcoming course in traditional craft. Work Study is part of North House's scholarship/accessibility program. See our Work Study Brochure by clicking here.

WORK STUDY OPTIONS:
WEDNESDAYS AT 1 PM YEARROUND & SPECIAL EVENTS
Setting up campus each week for an upcoming weekend of classes is a huge task, and we can always use help! Expect to earn 2-3 hours of course credit per week. In addition, there are many tasks before, during and after special events that qualify for Work Study credit. Applications are welcome anytime.

- Click here for complete Wednesday Work Study program description

WEEK-LONG SESSIONS
Work at North House for a week; stay in Grand Marais for free! During our “peak season” – May-November – we host a Work Study participant each week. Applications are welcome anytime. Sessions are based on the campus needs; sessions start on Tuesday at 1pm, and end the following Monday at noon. See session dates below. Session assignment for Spring/Summer sessions will begin March 1, and assignments for Late-Summer/Fall sessions will begin June 1. Expect to earn 24-32+ hours of Work Study credit during each session.
