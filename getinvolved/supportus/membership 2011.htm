Current Giving Opportunities

 Thank you for your interest in North House Folk School and our educational efforts!  North House is a 501-c3 educational nonprofit and there are three great ways to get involved in the coming year. Find out how to support North House below.

1. Get the Match
 Two lead donors have stepped forward to double
your donation to North House in Nov and Dec 2010!

 • NEW $5,000 matching gift for new donors, or anyone expanding their annual
donation to North House. Visit our annual support/membership page, download
our Craft Your Support Flyer and mail it to North House, or have your credit card
ready and call 218-387-9762 to donate--don't forget to mention the match!

 • MATCH MET $10,000 matching gift for new donors, or anyone expanding their annual donation to North House. Visit our annual support/membership page, download our Craft Your Support Flyer and mail it to North House, or have your credit card ready and call 218-387-9762 to donate--don't forget to mention the match!
• MATCH MET $10,000 matching gift for any donations designated for the
Raise The Roof campaign. More information available here.

2. Give To The Max
 Championed by GiveMN.org, Give to the Max Day Nov 16
is a celebration of Minnesota’s diverse nonprofit organizations
and the power of individual donors.

• Get Matched. Organizations with matching funds will be highlighted
on the GiveMN website, and two lead donors have stepped forward to
make your contribution to North House grow (see opportunity #1 above).

 • Get a Golden Ticket. One donor on GiveMN will be randomly chosen
every hour to have $1000 added to their donation. It could be you!

 • Win the Grand Prize. The two nonprofits in greater Minnesota that
attract the largest number of individual donors on Nov 16 will receive $20,000
and $10,000 grand prizes. Even a $5 or $10 donation will make a difference.
To donate through GiveMN.org click here

3. Sustain The Dream
 Become a North House Sustaining Member and help sustain the dream.
Monthly scheduled contributions are automatically withdrawn
from your credit card during the first week of every month.
Choose between a single year or an automatically renewable contribution.
Solid, consistent support to sustain the dream. To sign up click here.
