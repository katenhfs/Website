WEB LINKS OVERVIEW
The North House Folk School provides web links on its web site with the intent of providing useful resources to its students, alumni, supporters, partners and other web site visitors. North House’s specific goals are to:
* provide event and destination information related directly
    to the Grand Marais & Cook County region of the North Shore
* assist students as they consider lodging/camping options
   during their visit to North House
* recognize and provide visibility for North House’s non-profit
   community and regional partners
* foster the awareness of regional nonprofit organizations
   that share North House’s educational interests and
   commitment to traditional northern crafts
* create an expanded awareness of "the North" and the
   people, traditions, features and events that have shaped it
   throughout history

Organizations that believe they can help North House accomplish these goals through the exchange of web site links are welcome to contact North House. Inquiries should be directed to info@northhouse.org. North House reserves the right to discontinue any link for any reason at its own discretion.
