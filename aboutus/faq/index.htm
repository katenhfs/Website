FAQs
(Frequently Asked Questions)

How do I get to North House?

From the South: DULUTH, MINNESOTA (110 miles): Take Hwy 61 Northeast to the Grand Marais city limits. As you come into town, North House is the first right after the Grand Marais Municipal Campground. North House is on the harbor at the base of 5th Avenue West. Turn toward the harbor at the driveway just before our “Yellow House”.
 
From the North: THUNDER BAY, ONTARIO (80 miles): Take Hwy 61 Southwest to the Grand Marais city limits. Proceed through town to the base of 5th Avenue West. Take a left on the driveway just past our "Yellow House".

From the West: ELY, MINNESOTA (90 miles): Take Hwy 1 to Hwy 61 at Little Marais. Head up the shore of Hwy 61 and follow the directions from Duluth (above).

From the East: Batten the hatches and set sail across Lake Superior. We are located at latitude 47N 74' 44 and longitude 90W 33' 82. Look for the flashing lights at either side of the entrance to the harbor, and proceed to the southwest corner of the harbor.

Where can I park?
North House currently has limited parking available in our upper parking lot. If this lot is full, or if we are restricting parking, street parking is available. If you're boating, let's talk.

When I get to North House, where do I go?
Please plan to arrive at North House fifteen minutes before your class is scheduled to begin. Check in at the School Store/Office in the north end of the blue (west) building. Staff will guide you to your classroom location.

What is the schedule for the class day?
Courses generally run from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m., with an hour-long break for lunch around noon. Some courses do have different hours which are specified in the course catalog.

Where do I eat lunch?
There are also many fine restaurants within walking distance, and Grand Marais has two grocery stores and a large food co-op. North House has a student commons area in the blue classroom building which offers space for students to fix and eat lunch on campus (refrigerator, stove, microwave, etc.).

Where can I find area information?
North House has numerous links for area information on its Links page.  The Grand Marais Area Tourism Association office is on Broadway Avenue downtown. You can call them at 888-922.5000 or www.grandmarais.com If you want to visit them in person, take Hwy 61 to the stoplight and go 1 1/2 blocks toward the lake, on your right.
